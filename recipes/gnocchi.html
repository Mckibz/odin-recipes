One-Pan Chicken Gnocchi

Image of One-Pan Chicken Gnocchi

This creamy chicken gnocchi cooks in one pan, from chicken to veggies to creamy sauce, and uses convenient store-bought gnocchi. Itâ€™s a versatile recipe, and can be the perfect meal to use up scraps of most vegetables.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 2(8 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Gnocchi

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 pound gnocchi

Vegetables

  • 2 cups bite-sized broccoli floretes
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into thin ribbons
  • 1 yellow squash, cut into half moons
  • 1/2 cup quartered mushrooms
  • 1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper

Cream Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons minced shallot(about 1 shallot)
  • 1 cup chicken broth/li>
  • 3/4 cup half-and-half
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, or more to taste
